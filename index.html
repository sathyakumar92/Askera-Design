1. Agreement & Services

These terms govern the services provided by ASKera Design ("Agency") to you ("Client"). The specific services, deliverables, and timelines will be outlined in a separate Scope of Work (SOW) document, which, upon agreement, forms part of this contract.

2. Payment Terms

Our standard payment structure is as follows:

  • An advance payment of 50% of the total project cost is required before any work begins. This is non-refundable.
  • The remaining 50% is due upon project completion, prior to the delivery of final, high-resolution source files.
  • All payments are to be made via bank transfer or the payment gateway provided.
  • All quoted fees are exclusive of Goods and Services Tax (GST). The applicable GST rate will be added to each invoice as per Indian law.

3. Revisions & Scope Creep

The project fee includes a set number of revision rounds (typically two) as defined in the SOW. Additional revisions or significant changes to the scope after project commencement will be subject to additional charges at our standard hourly rate.

4. Intellectual Property Rights

Upon receipt of full and final payment, the Agency grants the Client full intellectual property rights for the final, approved design. The Agency retains the right to display the work in its portfolio, website, and other promotional materials for marketing purposes.

5. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep all confidential information, including business strategies and proprietary data, private and not to disclose it to any third party.

6. Termination

If the Client chooses to terminate the project, the initial 50% advance is forfeited. If the project is more than 50% complete at the time of termination, a pro-rated fee for work completed will be assessed and invoiced.
