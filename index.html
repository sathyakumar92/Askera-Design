Client Agreement Highlights

We believe in transparency. Here are the key points of our standard client agreement. A detailed contract will be provided upon project initiation.

1. Project Scope

All work is defined in a Project Proposal. Any tasks requested beyond the agreed scope will be considered extras and billed accordingly.

2. Payment Terms

A non-refundable 50% deposit is required to start. The final 50% is due upon completion, before delivery of final, high-resolution files.

3. Revision Rounds

Your project includes up to three rounds of minor revisions. Major changes to the core design concept after approval may incur additional charges.

4. Ownership & Rights

Upon final payment, you own the full copyright of the approved final designs. We retain the right to showcase the work in our portfolio.

5. Cancellation

If a project is cancelled, the initial deposit is non-refundable. The client will be billed for any work completed beyond the deposit amount.

6. Confidentiality

We agree to protect your confidential information. Likewise, our proprietary business processes and documents are to remain confidential.
